PinMapper Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Kirway LLC ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the PinMapper mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photos and Location Data

  • PinMapper accesses your photo library only with your explicit permission
  • We extract GPS coordinates embedded in your photos' metadata
  • All photo processing occurs locally on your device
  • We do not store, transmit, or access the actual photo content

1.2 Location Information

  • GPS coordinates from photo metadata
  • Location names derived from reverse geocoding
  • Custom location names you create
  • Categories and notes you add to locations

1.3 Usage Data

  • App performance and crash reports (anonymized)
  • Feature usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Subscription and purchase information (processed by Apple)

1.4 Device Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and settings preferences
  • Unique device identifiers for subscription management

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Create location pins from your photo metadata
  • Display your locations on interactive maps
  • Organize locations with custom categories
  • Export your data in various formats (Premium feature)

2.2 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Debug and fix technical issues
  • Develop new functionality based on user needs

2.3 Subscription Management

  • Process premium subscription purchases
  • Manage free trial periods
  • Restore purchases across devices

3. Data Storage and Processing

3.1 Local Storage

  • All location data is stored locally on your device using Core Data
  • Photos remain in your device's photo library
  • No location data is transmitted to external servers

3.2 iCloud Sync (Premium Feature)

  • Premium users can enable iCloud sync
  • Data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time

3.3 Data Retention

  • Data remains on your device until you delete the app
  • iCloud data follows Apple's iCloud retention policies
  • You can export or delete your data at any time

4. Information Sharing

4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

  • We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information
  • Your location data is never shared with third parties
  • Photo content never leaves your device

4.2 Service Providers

  • Apple App Store for payment processing
  • Apple iCloud for premium sync features (if enabled)
  • No other third-party services have access to your data

4.3 Legal Requirements

  • We may disclose information if required by law
  • We will notify you of such requests unless prohibited
  • We will challenge overly broad requests

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Data Access

  • View all your stored location data within the app
  • Export your data in CSV, JSON, or GPX formats (Premium)
  • Access your data through iOS Settings > Privacy

5.2 Data Control

  • Delete individual locations or categories
  • Clear all app data through iOS Settings
  • Disable photo access permissions at any time

5.3 Subscription Control

  • Cancel subscriptions through Apple's App Store
  • Request refunds according to Apple's policies
  • Manage subscription settings in your Apple ID account

6. Children's Privacy

PinMapper is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Security

7.1 Data Protection

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • iCloud data is encrypted using Apple's security standards
  • We implement industry-standard security practices

7.2 No Account Required

  • PinMapper does not require user accounts
  • No passwords or personal identifiers are collected
  • Your privacy is protected by design

8. International Users

PinMapper is designed for global use. Location data processing occurs locally regardless of your location. If you use iCloud sync, data storage follows Apple's international data policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing in-app notifications for significant changes
